Yoga Days

Yoga Days is an international event that is happening all over the world for 3 days.

3 days of FUN

During these 3 days you can get knowledge about yoga practising, try some meals that supports practicing yoga. There is also possible to attend many classes, listen to relaxing music, talk with other people interested in yoga as well as everything that is connected with environment.

While practicing you mostly have to discover what feelings are coming to you, understand them as well and try to use in the very best way later. If it's happiness - share it, if it's sadness- let yourself feel it and accept it, if it's excitement- go and do something creative. That's why meeting with other helps a lot in discovering yourself, try it and come for next Yoga Days !
