Food as important as excercises

by Magdalena Polakowska

There is a lot of rules that you should take under condsideration when it comes to getting full experience connected with wellness. Many people are talking about The Elements Rules when it comes to cooking. Every one of us is one kind of Elements, fire, air, earth or water. If you are a Fire one, you shouldn't eat for example spicy food beacuse you're an energetic person and after eating spicy things you can get more angry than usual. If you are an earth person you shouldn't eat some spices as well beacuse you'll be too sleepy or lazy.

Food can really change the way in which we feel and answer everyday challenges, we should eat food that will balance our characters.
